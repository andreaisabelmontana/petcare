🐾 PetCare

Python · SQLite · Domain analytics

Caring for pets is a team effort.

PetCare is a household pet-management core in Python: a SQLite-backed store for pets, members, activity logs and recurring reminders — plus the analytics that make the data useful: age, weight trends, what's due, and medication adherence. Standard library only, with a tested CLI.

01 — Features

One household, every pet, all in sync.

🏠

Households & members

A household with members and a unique join code; pets belong to a household.

🐕

Pets

Name, species, breed, birthdate and weight — full CRUD, persisted to SQLite.

📒

Activity logs

Timestamped feeding, walk, medication, weight and vet-visit entries per pet.

⏰

Reminders

Fixed-interval schedules (meds every 12h, annual shots) with a "what's due" query.

📈

Analytics

Age from birthdate, weight-trend detection, next-due dates, medication adherence.

⌨️

CLI

add-pet · log · due · stats over a SQLite database file.

02 — Analytics & tests

The logic that makes the data useful.

Pure functions over the store turn raw entries into answers: how old is the pet, is it gaining or losing weight, what's due now, and were the meds actually given? Each is covered by the pytest suite.
